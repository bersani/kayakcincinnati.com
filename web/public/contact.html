Directions

Directions from 275 East

(coming from the North side of Cincinnati)

  • From I-275 take Exit 57, OH 28 Milford/Blanchester
  • Go Right on State Rt. 28 towards Milford
  • Stay on Rt. 28 until you cross the Little Miami River (approximately 2.1 miles) and then go left at light on Rt 50.
  • Make your first left on Ferry Street
  • Kayak Cincinnati is at the bottom of the hill.

Directions from 275 North

(coming from the East side of Cincinnati or Northern Kentucky)

  • From I-275 take Exit 57, OH 28 Milford/Blanchester
  • Go Left on State Rt. 28 towards Milford
  • Stay on Rt. 28 until you cross the Little Miami River (approximately 2.1 miles) and then go left at light on Rt 50.
  • Make your first left on Ferry Street
