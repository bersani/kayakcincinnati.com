Trip Reminders

  • While kayaking it’s a good idea to wear old clothes and shoes, water shoes or sandals with straps.
  • You may want to bring a change of clothes and keep them in your car — Milford has some great shops and restaurants that are a worth checking out after your trip.
  • Bring plenty of sunscreen and have straps on glasses and sunglasses.
  • Please don’t take you car keys, wallet, or any valuables that may get lost or damaged by water. Check your keys at the livery.
  • Remember to wear your lifejacket at all times for your safety.
  • No Styrofoam coolers or glass bottles are permitted on the Little Miami River since it is a State and National Scenic River.
  • Please respect all private property and any others using the river for recreation.
  • Alcohol and water sports such as kayaking DON’T MIX. Alcohol is not permitted on the Little Miami. The Ohio Department of Natural Resources takes a strong stance against drinking alcoholic beverages in public and while on the river. Fines for open containers are up to $135.
