Kayak Cincinnati

Kayak Rental Trips on the Little Miami River

Kayak Cincinnati is conveniently located less than 20 minutes from downtown Cincinnati and Northern Kentucky. Our kayak livery is on the National and State Scenic Little Miami River near the town of Historic Milford in Jim Terrell Park. We will provide you with the finest quality rental equipment for your trip including a great selection of new kayaks from Jackson Kayak, Caviness paddles and clean, comfortable kayak style lifejackets that you will actually want to wear. Our unique trips will take you along what is regarded as some of the best kayaking water on the the Little Miami. You will experience beautiful scenery and interesting wildlife along the way as you relax and enjoy your time on the river.

Our goal is to provide you with the very best paddling experience possible. We rent kayaks and only kayaks at this time.
